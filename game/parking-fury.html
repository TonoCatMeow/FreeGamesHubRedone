Instructions

Tap and rotate the wheel to turn your car. Tap the gas pedal to go forward and the brake to go back. Follow the arrows and park your car inside the yellow rectangle.

Use WASD or the arrow keys to drive and turn your car. Drive your car into the highlighted parking space and move on to the next car. Don't hit the walls or other parked cars!

Who made Parking Fury?

Parking Fury was created by Ukrainian game developer Andriy Pidvirnyy. The game was released in 2016, and it has been a fan favorite on Coolmath Games since it was released on our site. The combination of skill, strategy, and spatial awareness makes for a fun and challenging game that has brought in tons of fans. 

How many levels does Parking Fury have?

Parking Fury only has 10 levels to run through. However, this does not mean that Parking Fury is an easy game by any standards. Each level grows in difficulty, and we think that you will find it to be quite difficult once you get to level 8 or 9. 

For some comparison, the fastest time ever on Parking Fury comes in at just around 7-and-a-half minutes according to the official speedrun website. This record is set by someone who has played Parking Fury dozens of times in order to get the fastest time possible. Beginners can probably expect Parking Fury to take around 20 to 30 minutes on the first attempt, although getting under 10 is certainly not out of reach with enough practice. 

Is this the only parking game on Coolmath Games?

Absolutely not! For starters, there are two other Parking Fury games here at Coolmath Games. Both are made by the same developer, Andriy Pidvirnyy. The games play very similarly to the first Parking Fury, just with new maps and a couple of new features. To check out those games, you can go to our Parking Fury games collection page. 

The Parking Fury series is not the only great example of a fun parking game on our site. Many players also enjoy the Parking Mania series. While currently there are only two non-flash games on our site right now, Parking Mania and Extreme Parking Mania 2, they both offer excellent gameplay if you are really in the mood to play some parking games. The mechanics are pretty much the same as Parking Fury. The Parking Mania series just has different maps and a slightly different look and feel. We recommend that you check them out once you have completed all levels from the Parking Mania series.
