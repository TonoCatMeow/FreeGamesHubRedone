Instructions

Press the roll button to earn points. Spend your points to upgrade your dice or buy new ones. More choices open up the longer you play.

Left click or press Space to roll, or just wait and let the auto-roll do the work (once you've unlocked it). Each time you roll you earn points, which you can spend to upgrade your dice or buy new ones. More items open up the longer you play, use them to make your dice more valuable.

They keep rolling even if you close the game, so when you come back you might have earned thousands of points or more! 

What is the appeal of Idle Dice?

Idle Dice is a great game for when you feel like playing a game without putting a ton of effort. While you will likely do better if you keep a close eye on the game so you can make upgrades when necessary, there is no shame in putting Idle Dice on another tab and checking in on occasion while you are doing something else. That’s the beautiful thing about idle games - they don’t need all of your love and attention in order to succeed in them.

What are some good strategies for Idle Dice?

The first tip that we have is that you should be spending your money as often as you can. Don’t just save up for the most expensive upgrade, it will be more efficient if you are just constantly upgrading whenever you have the ability to. 

When you have the means, make sure to get an extra dice too. The more dice you have, the more money you will get. The biggest benefit is that the extra dice will give you more opportunities to get a pair with another dice and double your points. The multipliers in this game are where the big money really comes from.

You should also be manually rolling the dice as much as you get at the beginning of the game to earn money. While you absolutely don’t have to, you would be surprised by how much more money you can earn when you click the dice instead of waiting for the automatic rolling timer to come into effect. 

Our last tip - before you hop off of Idle Dice, make sure you spend all of the money you can. The game will continue to play, even when you hop off. You might as well get as good of a setup as possible before you leave so you can maximize the money you earn while you are gone.

What are some games similar to Idle Dice?

Idle Dice is on a long list of Idle Games that we have here on Coolmath. If you want to check out our Idle Games Playlist, you can click right here.

Idle Breakout is especially similar to Idle Dice. Idle Breakout is basically the same thing as Idle Dice, except players are earning money by purchasing increasingly destructive balls. The game has a very similar look and feel to Idle Dice, and we’d be surprised if anyone only liked one of these games instead of both.
